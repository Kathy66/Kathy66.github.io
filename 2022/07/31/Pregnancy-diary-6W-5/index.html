Pregnancy diary 6W+5

发表于 2022-07-31 | 分类于 生活

Hi my boy/girl,
This is a ‘heartquake’ week. Before I meet ‘you’ by B-ultrasound, I found pale pink blood stains when I urinated. At that moment I felt a little flustered, and I want to talk
with someone who has had experience. First, I looked into information on the internet, it seems not a big problem if I didn’t bleeding all the time. But these information can’t erase my nervous, I can help to consulting a experienced colleague. I keep hinting myself, you are ok and I will see you tomorrow.
Thanks god, when I go to hospital this thursday, I found fetal heart,fetal buds and fertilized egg, meanwhile,my leucorrhea turns normal that day. The doctor says my embryo(means
‘you’) are heathy. As the pale pink blood stains, I can keep observing. When I got my second progesterone test results, it’s approximately 24 ng/ml(big unit, 1ng/ml=3.18nmol/L). So I think this issue will not reappear in high probability. Your dad also very happy when I told him the result.
Noon on Friday，I found pale pink blood stains again and it’s quantity even more than first time found. I was flustered again and came up idea go to hospital. It’s really make me
nervous when any abnormal situation appear. To be honest, I never thought that I will have these cautious emotions before pergnancy. The problem of intermittent bleeding persisted until this morning, I felt a little relieved this afternoon. However, I’m not sure will this situation happen again and again in the coming weeks?
My dear, your father and I looking forward for your coming, please keep healthy and strong.
